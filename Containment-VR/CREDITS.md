# Containment — Credits

All third-party assets used in Containment are CC0 / public domain.

## Audio (CC0)

- UI haptic-tick / button click — Kenney Interface Sounds (kenney.nl, CC0)
- Cable plug-in click — freesound.org (CC0 filter)
- Door servo / unlock — freesound.org (CC0 filter)
- Ambient lab hum — freesound.org (CC0 filter)
- Dial detent tick — freesound.org (CC0 filter)

## Graphics

Geometry is procedurally generated from Unity primitives inside
`ContainmentSetupMenu.cs`. No third-party 3D models are required to ship.
Optional polish (recommended CC0 sources):

- ambientCG — wall and floor textures
- Poly Haven — props and HDRIs
